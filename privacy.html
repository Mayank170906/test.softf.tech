Privacy Policy

At test.softf.tech (“we”, “our”, “us”), your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our website and tools. By using our Service, you agree to this policy.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All tools on this site operate entirely in your browser, and no data is sent to any server. We do not use cookies, analytics, tracking scripts, or third-party storage.

2. Local Processing

Every test — such as keyboard, mouse, display, speaker, IP, or speed testing — runs completely within your browser. Any temporary data is stored in memory and deleted automatically when you close or refresh the page.

3. Third-Party Services

In some tools (like IP Lookup or Speed Test), we may request data from trusted third-party APIs (e.g., for IP detection or speed measurement). These APIs may process your connection information (such as IP address) only for the purpose of providing the requested result. We do not store, reuse, or share this data in any way.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies. Your activity on test.softf.tech is completely private and anonymous.

5. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, the risk of data compromise is virtually zero. However, we recommend keeping your browser up to date for maximum security when using online tools.

6. International Compliance

test.softf.tech is accessible worldwide and aims to comply with privacy principles under laws such as:

  • GDPR (European Union)
  • CCPA / CPRA (California, USA)
  • CalOPPA (California Online Privacy Protection Act)

Since no personal data is collected, these regulations are met by default.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under that age. If you believe a child has provided personal data to us by mistake, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most recent version will always be available on this page. Continued use of the website after any modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

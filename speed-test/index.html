Understanding the Metrics

IP Address

Your IP (Internet Protocol) address is a unique identifier for your device on the internet, much like a mailing address for your house. It allows data to be sent to and from your computer correctly.

Ping (Latency)

Ping measures the round-trip time for a small piece of data to travel from your computer to a server and back. A lower ping (in milliseconds) means a more responsive connection, which is crucial for online gaming and video calls.
